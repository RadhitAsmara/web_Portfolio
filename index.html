About Us

Who Am I?

Hello, I'm Radhitya Kurnia Asmara. I actively participate in national Cybersecurity competitions, especially "Capture The Flag" (CTF). CTF has taught me to overcome challenges, deepening my understanding of cybersecurity essentials. My main focus is Cybersecurity, Backend Development and Networking. I am excited to continue improving my cybersecurity skills through CTF and the Cybersecurity community of SMK Negeri 7 Semarang, known as STEMBACTF.

Participating in CTF has sharpened my skills in addressing cybersecurity challenges. I've tackled obstacles using Kali Linux, developed Python and C scripts, and utilized diverse tools. This approach encourages knowledge sharing and a collaborative ethos in dealing with digital security matters. Motivated by a strong learning inclination, my goal is to make positive strides in the realm of IT and cybersecurity.
